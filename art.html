Before the Skies Turned Gray

I painted this while listening to the song Jet Pilot by System of a Down and the music definitely inspired the painting. Building from the lyrics of the song, I showed gathering clouds and a stark black line that represents a jet plane.


Purple Desert Darkness

This one was also inspired by music, though if I honest I don't exactly recall what music. It may have been the album OK Computer by Radiohead. There's a cold beauty to that album, which I think comes across in the painting.
